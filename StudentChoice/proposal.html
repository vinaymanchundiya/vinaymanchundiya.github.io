  • Introduction

    Human Anatomy Atlas 2018, is a multiplatform software which incorporates sophisticated models of human body parts and presents it in the form a Human body on the table top scale. The AR feature of this application is only available for handheld devices (iOS and Android). The application comprises of the following body systems - nervous, skeletal, circulatory, muscular, digestive, urinary, lymphatic, endocrine, and reproductive. Along with the visualisation of human body, the application also includes an encyclopedia refernece and study content in different languages. If some student in the healthcare domain were to study all the body systems then such an application comes very handy.
