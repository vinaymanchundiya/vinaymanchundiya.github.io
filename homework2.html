Few words on this Technology

Initially when I began working on this Technology, I was kind of excited, as I will be learning various interactions between different components(such as models and geometry of the real world). IDEs have made things simple. As simple as drag and drop OR plug and play. Even though the character appeared correctly above the Image Target, it made me sad as I could not really infer what actually happened in the background(apart from Image Feature Extraction by Vuforia). This made me realise that it is easy to be well trained with the IDE but a through background knowledge is equally important. To start with, understanding the difference between all the world center modes will suffice. I am pretty sure about one thing though, Vuforia and Unity are going to make the life simpler. If I can get myself well trained with the combo, I can do pretty cool things together. May be build up a AR pet or a cool game. This will be even more fun if I can keep my hands free and still enjoy AR. This is just the beginning of what could come.
